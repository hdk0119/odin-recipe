Smoked Salmon Sushi

Description

Salmon sushi rolls are surprisingly easy to make at home! You just need a good salmon sushi recipe â€” and that's where we come in.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi paste
  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice, then mix in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate.
  2. Assemble the sushi rolls.
  3. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces.
